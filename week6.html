About Our Initiative

Connecting Universities Through Chess

The University Chess Network was established in 2022 to foster connections between collegiate chess communities across the nation. What began as friendly matches between neighboring schools has grown into a nationwide network of university chess families.

"Our mission is to promote chess as a vehicle for intellectual growth, strategic thinking, and cross-campus collaboration while celebrating the rich diversity of university chess cultures."

We organize regular inter-university matches, provide resources for developing chess programs, and create opportunities for chess enthusiasts to connect across institutional boundaries.
